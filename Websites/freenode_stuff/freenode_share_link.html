How to Share a Link on Freenode

To Share C++ Code:


1. Go to: https://wandbox.org

2. Type your code then hit the share button

3. There should be a share button and a URL option to get address

4. Copy URL and paste it on text input line in HexChat, or some other such


To Share A Screen Shot or Other Image:


1. Post image on ibb.co, or some other such

2. Copy URL and paste it on text input line in HexChat, or some other such
